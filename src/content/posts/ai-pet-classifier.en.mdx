# The Vision
Working with pet shelters showed me a common problem: staff often have to guess breeds by eye, which can be inaccurate for mixed-breed animals. I wanted to build a tool that uses **Deep Learning** to provide more accurate classifications.

## Technical Details
I used **Transfer Learning** on a pre-trained ResNet model:
- **Dataset**: Over 5,000 images of various dog and cat breeds.
- **Framework**: PyTorch.
- **Fine-tuning**: We unfroze the top layers and trained specifically on our target species.

## Result
The model achieved **94% accuracy** on the validation set, providing a reliable tool for shelter volunteers.
